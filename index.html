Ant Infestations

Ants are incredibly common in West Haven and can quickly become a major nuisance. They enter homes seeking food and water, leaving scent trails that guide others to the source. You might see trails of ants across your countertops, floors, or even in pantries. While most common ants are harmless, some species can bite or sting. More importantly, their presence indicates potential accessible food sources within your home. Our ant control services go beyond simply spraying visible ants; we identify the colony source and use targeted treatments to eliminate the entire colony, preventing future invasions.

Rodent Problems

Mice and rats are not only unsettling to find in your home, but they also pose significant health risks and can cause considerable property damage. Chewed wires, gnawed wood, droppings, and scratching sounds in walls or attics are all signs of a rodent infestation. Rodents can carry diseases and contaminate food surfaces. Our rodent control services involve safe and effective methods for trapping and removing rodents, as well as identifying and sealing entry points to prevent them from returning. We also provide recommendations for sanitizing affected areas to mitigate health risks.

Termite Activity

Termites are among the most destructive pests homeowners face. They silently feed on wood, causing significant structural damage that can go unnoticed for years. Signs of termites include discarded wings near windowsills or doors, mud tubes on foundations, damaged wood that sounds hollow when tapped, and bubbling or peeling paint. Early detection and intervention are crucial to minimize damage. Our termite treatment services utilize advanced techniques to eliminate termite colonies and protect your home from future infestations. A thorough inspection is the first step in determining if you have a termite problem.

Cockroach Sightings

Cockroaches are resilient and can quickly multiply, posing significant health risks as they can contaminate food and spread bacteria. They are often found in kitchens and bathrooms, hiding in cracks and crevices during the day and becoming active at night. Seeing cockroaches, finding their droppings (which resemble coffee grounds or black pepper), or noticing a musty odor are all indicators of an infestation. Our cockroach control services involve targeted treatments to eliminate current infestations and strategies to prevent their return, addressing both the insects and the conditions that attract them.

Spider Concerns

While most spiders are harmless and can even be beneficial by controlling other insects, some species found in West Haven can be venomous and pose a threat. Seeing an increase in spiders, finding webs in various locations, or encountering large spiders indoors can be concerning. Our spider removal services safely remove spiders and address the factors that are attracting them to your home, such as other insect infestations. We can also provide recommendations for reducing spider habitats around your property.

Bed Bug Infestations

Bed bugs are notorious for causing itchy bites and being extremely difficult to eliminate. They hide in mattresses, box springs, furniture, and cracks in walls, coming out at night to feed. Signs of bed bugs include small, reddish-brown stains on bedding or furniture, shed skins, tiny white eggs or egg casings, and receiving unexplained bites. Bed bug infestations require professional treatment due to their resilience and ability to hide effectively. Our bed bug extermination services utilize proven methods to eliminate bed bugs from your home and provide guidance on preventing future infestations.

Mosquito Problems

Mosquitoes are not just annoying; they can also transmit diseases like West Nile Virus and Eastern Equine Encephalitis in Connecticut. They breed in standing water, making properties with puddles, bird baths, or clogged gutters potential breeding grounds. Excessive mosquitoes around your home or experiencing numerous bites outdoors are clear indicators of a mosquito problem. Our mosquito control services target mosquito breeding sites and reduce adult mosquito populations, allowing you to enjoy your outdoor spaces again. We can also provide advice on eliminating standing water sources around your property.

Bee and Wasp Activities

While bees are essential pollinators, their nests near homes can pose a risk, especially for individuals with allergies. Wasps, on the other hand, can be aggressive and sting multiple times. Finding a bee or wasp nest on your property requires careful handling to ensure safety. Our bee and wasp removal services safely remove nests and address the reasons why these stinging insects are attracted to your property. We prioritize humane removal of bees when possible.
